Surface Area of a Sphere

Surface Area = 4πr²
The surface area equals 4 times the area of a circle with the same radius

Question D2: Painting the Moon

A theme park builds a Moon model

• Sphere radius = 21 meters
• Paint thickness = 1 mm = 0.001 m
• How many liters of paint needed?

Answer D2: Painting the Moon

1. Surface Area = 4 × (22/7) × 21 × 21
= 4 × 22 × 63
= 5544 m²
2. Paint volume = Surface Area × Thickness
= 5544 × 0.001
= 5.544 m³
3. Convert to liters: 5.544 × 1000
= 5544 liters

Volume of a Sphere

Volume of Sphere = (4/3)πr³
Volume of Hemisphere = (2/3)πr³

Question E2: Ice Cream Scoop

Calculate the volume of a spherical ice cream scoop

• Radius = 21 mm

Answer E2: Ice Cream Scoop

V = (4/3) × π × r³
V = (4/3) × (22/7) × 21³
V = (4/3) × (22/7) × 9261
= (4 × 22 × 9261) / (3 × 7)
= (88 × 9261) / 21
= 814968 / 21
= 38,808 mm³
(≈ 38.8 ml)
