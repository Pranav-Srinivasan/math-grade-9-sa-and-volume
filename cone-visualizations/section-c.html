Big Cone (Complete)

The Waffle Basket

Question C1

Find the Total Surface Area

(Including the circular base at the bottom)

The Waffle Basket

Answer C1: Total Surface Area

  1. Big cone CSA = (22/7) × 42 × 70 = 9240 mm²
  2. Small cone CSA = (22/7) × 21 × 35 = 2310 mm²
  3. Frustum CSA = 9240 − 2310 = 6930 mm²
  4. Base area = (22/7) × 21² = 1386 mm²
  5. Total Surface Area = 6930 + 1386 = 8316 mm²

The Waffle Basket

Question C2

Find the volume the basket can hold

The Waffle Basket

Answer C2: Volume

  1. Big cone volume = ⅓ × (22/7) × 42² × 56 = 103,488 mm³
  2. Small cone volume = ⅓ × (22/7) × 21² × 28 = 12,936 mm³
  3. Frustum volume = 103,488 − 12,936 = 90,552 mm³
Ratio check: Big cone / Small cone = 8 (since 2³ = 8)
Frustum = 7 × Small cone volume ✓
